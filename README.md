# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective

To create interactive visualizations and derive business insights from a marketing campaign dataset using Power BI.

## Dashboard Title

Marketing Campaign Customer Insights Dashboard

## Visualizations Created

* KPI Cards (Total Customers, Average Income, Total Spending)
* Customer Distribution by Education
* Customer Distribution by Marital Status
* Income vs Customer Spending
* Customer Spending by Product Category
* Marketing Campaign Performance

## Business Insights

* Customer demographics were analyzed using education and marital status.
* Spending patterns were analyzed across product categories.
* Relationship between customer income and spending was visualized.
* Marketing campaign effectiveness was compared.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel

## Outcome

Successfully created a professional dashboard to communicate customer insights and marketing campaign performance.
